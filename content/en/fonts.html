On this page, you will find my fonts. I'm not at all a professional font creator, so don't expect too much of them.

Blob

Blob is a ‘blobby’ font for headers, slogans and such; it is not meant for paragraphs of text. It has support for the (basic) Latin and Greek scripts, and some (especially mathematical) symbols. I made this font in the beginning of 2012 using FontForge. Blob is licensed under the CC0 (public domain).

Examples

Blob is a 'blobby' font
In nova fert animus mutatas dicere formas
Μηνιν αειδε, θεα, Πηληϊαδεω Αχιληος
∀ n ∈ ℕ : ∃ m ∈ ℕ : m > n

Wimiso

Wimiso is a font I created from my block-letter handwriting. Normally, I write in cursive, but that is horrible to convert to a font. I made this font in 2015 by using the auto-trace feature in FontForge. It supports

  • the Latin script (including the Latin-1 supplement and large parts of Latin Extended-A);
  • the Greek script (including polytonic);
  • most of the Tengwar (encoded using the CSUR proposal).

Note that the position of diacritics is buggy; they may look bad with some of the letters. For the Tengwar, the tehta positioning is especially bad, and there seems to be no easy way to fix this.

Wimiso is licensed under the CC0 (public domain).

Examples

Some maths: 2 ÷ 4 = ½ and 21 × 3 = 63
